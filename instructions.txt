Old Scotch Shortbread

Ingredients

3/4 cup butter
1/4 cup shortening
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking powder
2 cups flour

1. Cream butter, shortening and sugar together. 
2. Add vanilla, baking powder and flour and mix.
3. Press into an 8 x 9 inch pan about 1 inch thick and prick with fork.
4. Bake for 30 minutes in a 300 degree oven.
5. Cut into slices when still warm.
6. When cool, put away in a sealed tin for about one month before eating.
